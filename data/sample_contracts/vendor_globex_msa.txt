MASTER SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Master Services Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of January 15, 2025, by and
between Klaus Compliance Solutions, Inc. ("Company") and Globex Systems, LLC ("Vendor"), for
the provision of managed IT infrastructure support services.

Section 1. Definitions

1.1 "Confidential Information" has the meaning set forth in Section 2.1.

1.2 "Services" means the managed infrastructure support services described in one or more
Statements of Work executed under this Agreement.

1.3 "Personal Data" means any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural
person processed by Vendor in the course of providing the Services.

Section 2. Confidentiality

2.1 "Confidential Information" means all non-public technical, business, or financial
information disclosed by one Party to the other in connection with this Agreement, including
system architecture, network configurations, and Company's internal policies.

2.2 The receiving Party shall protect the disclosing Party's Confidential Information using at
least the same degree of care it uses to protect its own confidential information of similar
importance, and in no event less than reasonable care.

2.3 Survival. The obligations of confidentiality under this Section 2 shall survive termination
of this Agreement for a period of two (2) years following the effective date of termination.

Section 3. Data Handling and Security

3.1 Vendor shall process Personal Data only as necessary to provide the Services and in
accordance with Company's written instructions.

3.2 Vendor shall maintain a written information security program that includes network
segmentation, access logging, and encryption of Personal Data at rest and in transit.

3.3 Breach Notification. Vendor shall notify Company of any confirmed unauthorized access to
Personal Data within five (5) business days of confirming the incident, and shall cooperate with
Company's investigation and any required regulatory notifications.

Section 4. Limitation of Liability

4.1 Each Party's aggregate liability arising out of this Agreement shall not exceed the total
fees paid or payable by Company to Vendor in the twelve (12) months preceding the event giving
rise to the claim.

4.2 The limitation in Section 4.1 does not apply to claims arising from a Party's gross
negligence, willful misconduct, or breach of Section 2 (Confidentiality).

Section 5. Term and Termination

5.1 This Agreement remains in effect for an initial term of twenty-four (24) months and renews
automatically for successive twelve (12) month terms unless either Party provides notice of
non-renewal at least sixty (60) days before the end of the then-current term.

5.2 Either Party may terminate this Agreement for convenience upon sixty (60) days' prior
written notice.

5.3 Company may terminate immediately for cause if Vendor fails to meet the service levels
specified in an applicable Statement of Work for two (2) consecutive calendar months.

Section 6. General Provisions

6.1 Governing Law. This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of New York.

6.2 Assignment. Neither Party may assign this Agreement without the prior written consent of
the other Party, except in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of substantially all
assets.
