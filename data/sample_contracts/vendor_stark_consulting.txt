CONSULTING SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Consulting Services Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of May 5, 2025, by and
between Klaus Compliance Solutions, Inc. ("Company") and Stark Strategic Consulting LLC
("Consultant"), for the provision of regulatory compliance advisory services.

Section 1. Definitions

1.1 "Confidential Information" means any non-public information disclosed by either Party in
connection with the Services, including Company's internal compliance policies, audit findings,
and strategic plans.

1.2 "Services" means the advisory and consulting services described in one or more Statements
of Work executed under this Agreement.

Section 2. Confidentiality

2.1 Consultant shall hold Company's Confidential Information in strict confidence and shall not
use it for any purpose other than performing the Services.

2.2 Consultant shall not disclose Company's Confidential Information to any subcontractor
without Company's prior written consent and a written confidentiality agreement at least as
protective as this Section 2.

2.3 Survival. The confidentiality obligations under this Section 2 shall survive termination of
this Agreement for a period of two (2) years.

Section 3. Data Handling and Security

3.1 To the extent Consultant accesses Company data in the course of performing the Services,
Consultant shall store such data on encrypted devices and shall not copy such data to personal
or unmanaged devices.

3.2 Breach Notification. Consultant shall notify Company within ten (10) business days of
discovering any unauthorized access to or loss of Company data in Consultant's possession.

Section 4. Limitation of Liability

4.1 Consultant's liability for damages arising out of or related to this Agreement, whether in
contract, tort, or otherwise, shall be unlimited and shall not be subject to any cap, in
consideration of the advisory nature of the Services and Consultant's role in Company's
regulatory compliance function.

4.2 Neither Party shall be liable for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages, except
where such damages arise from Consultant's breach of Section 2 (Confidentiality) or Consultant's
gross negligence.

Section 5. Term and Termination

5.1 This Agreement remains in effect for the duration of the active Statement(s) of Work, and
in any event no longer than twelve (12) months from the effective date unless extended by
written agreement of the Parties.

5.2 Either Party may terminate this Agreement for convenience upon thirty (30) days' prior
written notice.

5.3 Company may terminate immediately for cause if Consultant materially breaches this
Agreement and fails to cure such breach within ten (10) days of written notice.

Section 6. General Provisions

6.1 Governing Law. This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of New York.

6.2 Independent Contractor. Consultant is an independent contractor and not an employee, agent,
or partner of Company.
