SOFTWARE-AS-A-SERVICE SUBSCRIPTION AGREEMENT

This Software-as-a-Service Subscription Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of
April 22, 2025, by and between Klaus Compliance Solutions, Inc. ("Company") and Umbrella
Software Corp. ("Vendor"), for Company's subscription to Vendor's cloud-based expense
management platform.

Section 1. Definitions

1.1 "Confidential Information" means non-public information disclosed by either Party in
connection with this Agreement, including Company's expense data, employee names, and financial
records uploaded to the platform.

1.2 "Platform" means Vendor's hosted expense management software, including all associated
data storage and processing infrastructure.

Section 2. Confidentiality

2.1 Each Party shall protect the other Party's Confidential Information using reasonable care
and shall not disclose it to third parties except as necessary to perform its obligations under
this Agreement.

2.2 Survival. The confidentiality obligations under this Section 2 shall survive termination of
this Agreement for a period of one (1) year.

Section 3. Data Handling and Security

3.1 Vendor shall maintain reasonable security measures for data uploaded to the Platform,
consistent with Vendor's general security practices for its multi-tenant hosting environment.

3.2 Breach Notification. If Vendor becomes aware of unauthorized access to Company data on the
Platform, Vendor will notify Company as soon as reasonably practicable following its internal
investigation and determination that notification is warranted. Vendor does not commit to a
specific notification timeframe, as the appropriate response time depends on the nature and
scope of each incident.

3.3 Vendor may engage third-party hosting providers to support the Platform without prior
notice to Company, provided such providers are contractually bound to maintain confidentiality.

Section 4. Limitation of Liability

4.1 Each Party's aggregate liability arising out of this Agreement shall not exceed seven
hundred fifty thousand dollars ($750,000), except for liability arising from a Party's breach
of Section 2 (Confidentiality), which shall not be subject to this limitation.

4.2 Neither Party shall be liable for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages.

Section 5. Term and Termination

5.1 This Agreement has an initial term of twelve (12) months and renews automatically for
successive twelve (12) month terms unless either Party provides notice of non-renewal.

5.2 Either Party may terminate this Agreement for convenience upon ninety (90) days' prior
written notice.

5.3 Company may terminate immediately if Vendor experiences an unresolved Platform outage
exceeding forty-eight (48) consecutive hours.

Section 6. General Provisions

6.1 Governing Law. This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of California.

6.2 Entire Agreement. This Agreement, together with any order forms executed by the Parties,
constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties regarding its subject matter.
