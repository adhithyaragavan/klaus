MUTUAL NON-DISCLOSURE AND VENDOR EVALUATION AGREEMENT

This Mutual Non-Disclosure and Vendor Evaluation Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of
March 3, 2025, by and between Klaus Compliance Solutions, Inc. ("Company") and Acme Corporation
("Vendor"), collectively the "Parties," in connection with Vendor's provision of enterprise
procurement software for evaluation by Company.

Section 1. Definitions

1.1 "Confidential Information" means any non-public information disclosed by either Party,
whether in written, oral, or electronic form, that is designated as confidential at the time of
disclosure or that a reasonable person would understand to be confidential given the nature of
the information and the circumstances of disclosure, including pricing, product roadmaps,
security architecture, and customer lists.

1.2 "Personal Data" means any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural
person that is processed by either Party in connection with this Agreement.

Section 2. Confidentiality

2.1 Each Party agrees to hold the other Party's Confidential Information in strict confidence
and not to disclose such Confidential Information to any third party without the prior written
consent of the disclosing Party, except to employees and contractors with a legitimate need to
know who are bound by confidentiality obligations at least as protective as those in this
Agreement.

2.2 Each Party agrees to use the other Party's Confidential Information solely for the purpose
of evaluating and administering the vendor relationship contemplated by this Agreement.

2.3 Survival. The confidentiality obligations set forth in this Section 2 shall survive
termination or expiration of this Agreement for a period of three (3) years, except with
respect to Confidential Information that constitutes a trade secret under applicable law, which
shall remain protected for as long as it retains trade secret status.

Section 3. Data Handling and Security

3.1 Vendor shall implement and maintain commercially reasonable administrative, technical, and
physical safeguards designed to protect Personal Data against unauthorized access, disclosure,
alteration, or destruction, consistent with industry standards for software vendors handling
enterprise procurement data.

3.2 Breach Notification. In the event of a Security Incident involving unauthorized access to
or disclosure of Personal Data, Vendor shall notify Company within seventy-two (72) hours of
Vendor becoming aware of the incident, and shall provide Company with all information reasonably
necessary for Company to meet its own regulatory notification obligations.

3.3 Vendor shall not transfer Personal Data outside the jurisdiction in which it was collected
without Company's prior written consent.

Section 4. Limitation of Liability

4.1 Except for breaches of Section 2 (Confidentiality) or a Party's indemnification obligations,
each Party's aggregate liability arising out of or related to this Agreement shall not exceed
five hundred thousand dollars ($500,000).

4.2 Neither Party shall be liable for any indirect, incidental, special, or consequential
damages, including lost profits, even if advised of the possibility of such damages.

Section 5. Term and Termination

5.1 This Agreement commences on the effective date and continues for twelve (12) months unless
earlier terminated in accordance with this Section 5.

5.2 Either Party may terminate this Agreement for convenience upon thirty (30) days' prior
written notice to the other Party.

5.3 Either Party may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written notice if the other
Party materially breaches this Agreement and fails to cure such breach within fifteen (15) days
of receiving written notice of the breach.

Section 6. General Provisions

6.1 Governing Law. This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Delaware, without
regard to its conflict of laws principles.

6.2 Entire Agreement. This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties with
respect to its subject matter and supersedes all prior agreements and understandings, whether
written or oral.
